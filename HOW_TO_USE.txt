Miki Word Document Formatter v1.0

HOW TO USE:
1. Double-click MikiFormatter.exe
2. Click "Select File" or "Select Folder" 
3. Click "Start Processing"
4. Done! Check the "success" folder for results

WHAT IT DOES:
- Adds total rows to Word tables
- Works with tables containing: Package, Service, Type, Purchased, Used, Remaining, Trend
- Original files are not modified

SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS:
- Windows (any version)
- No additional software needed

If you have problems, contact technical support.
